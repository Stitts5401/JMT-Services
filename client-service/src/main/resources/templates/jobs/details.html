Contract Agreement Policy:

The client will receive a contract agreement via email within 48 hours of successful job request submission.

In case the requested start date is unavailable, an alternate schedule according to the client‚Äôs preference will be arranged and a new contract agreement will be sent via email.

Alternatively, the client may choose to cancel the job request before the contract agreement is signed and no fee will be charged.

Cancellation Policy:
  • More than 30 days before the start date: No charge
  • 15 to 30 days before the start date: 25% of the estimated job cost
  • 7 to 14 days before the start date: 50% of the estimated job cost
  • Less than 7 days before the start date: 75% of the estimated job cost
  • After job commencement: 100% of the estimated job cost

All charges are subject to taxes as applicable. Estimates are valid for 30 days and subject to change without prior notice.

Refund Policy:

Any applicable refund amount will be processed within 15 business days after the cancellation request is confirmed.

Refunds will be made via the original payment method or a company check.
